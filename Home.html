The issue as a whole

Gender Inequality and discrimination are prominent issues in today's world that are continuing to grow and impacting the lives of both males and females all around the world. This issue exists in the lives of not only women, but also men facing unjust stereotypes like being the breadwinner, further perpetuating the issue.

A Variety of data proves the existence of this issue in our society. This issue is prevalent in our local society and also greatly impacts the global economy, for a variety of reasons. According to sources, Gender-based discrimination in social institutions costs the world economy $6 trillion according to the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development. Moreover, this issue continues to perpetuate and impact the lives of global citizens across the globe. This is evident as, Out of 190 countries surveyed, the World Bank found that women were on equal legal standing with men in just 12 countries.

The world gender discrimination describes not only one issue but a variety of issues hidden within the branch. Girls are exposed to the prospect of child marriage, adolescent pregnancy, child domestic employment, poor education and health, sexual abuse, exploitation, and violence as a result of the presence of gender discrimination and social norms and practises. Many of these manifestations will persist unless girls and the gender discrimination as a whole issue are given more value. Boys are also a subject to a variety of gender related stereotypes like, Men don't do household chores; Men should be the financial providers for their family.
